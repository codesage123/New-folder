ABOUT CFG

CFG Travel is about uncovering and sharing the most authentic cultural experiences our city has to offer. From adrenaline-ﬁlled adventure to the subtle indulgence of the senses, we’re committed to taking visitors on a journey through the urban suburbs and showcasing the BIG 7 Tourism sites Cape Town has to offer, making sure you leave with stories no-one else can tell. We aim to give visitors a true taste of South Africa through rich Cape Cultural Experiences. Our experiences are suitable for Couples, Friends & Families and Group. When TRAVELING with CFG we place more value on People before Places.

 

“We so unique… when you leave the Cape… the Cape never Leaves You”

 

 

OVERVIEW- introductions

Discover one of the most beautiful Cities in the World, with flawless beaches exquisite sceneries and smiling faces everywhere you go.  Our awesome backdrop “Table Mountain” towering and cradling our rich Historical Gems and melting pot of cultures. We believe that all visitors should have the opportunity to experience the true richness of Cape Town. We also believe visitors are not just here to see sights, they are here to meet the people…. At CFG we put “People before Places” Our Capeflats Guide and Ambassadors are enthusiastic explorers who moves and interacts with different cultures with ease and passion. They always have new experiences to share and everyone they come in contact with is taken on a journey through their words and vibrant expression. Authenticity and discovery are their greatest loves.
